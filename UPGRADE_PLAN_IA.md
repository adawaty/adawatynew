# Adawaty Website Upgrade — IA + Requirements (2026-03-03)

## Target audiences (approachable)
- **Lawyers**: credibility, authority, lead capture, appointment intake, case-type filtering, local SEO.
- **Doctors / Clinics**: trust + outcomes, booking, clinic locations, services, reviews, content that answers patient questions.
- **Business owners / executives**: clarity, demand, operational systems, reporting, scalable workflows.

## Offers to add / highlight
1. **App development** (web + mobile)
   - MVP build, client portals, internal ops tools, integrations.
2. **Bio pages** (personal brand / “link-in-bio” hub)
   - Credibility stack, media kit, lead capture, scheduling, WhatsApp.
3. **Scheduling + intake**
   - Booking flows, confirmations, no-show reduction, forms, routing.
4. **ERP / internal systems**
   - Lightweight ERP around *your* workflows: approvals, inventory, CRM, pipelines, dashboards.

- Sprint 0: Discovery + scope lock
- Sprint 1: Brand + architecture
- Sprint 2: Design + prototype
- Sprint 3: Build + tracking
- Sprint 4: Launch + handover + growth plan

## Animation/interaction goals (“scroll stoppers”)
- Hero: kinetic typography + parallax background + stagger reveal.
- A pinned/sticky section (one screen that animates through 3–5 “chapters” on scroll).
- Scroll-driven diagram/line (Brand→Build→Demand becomes animated on scroll).
- Micro-interactions: cards, buttons, nav, pricing toggles.

- Structured data: Organization, WebSite, **Service**, **FAQPage**, BreadcrumbList.
- Add `llms.txt` with short, structured site summary + key URLs.
